Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I create a new account?

Visit our homepage and click on "Sign Up". Fill in the required details to register a new account.

2. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" at the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

3. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@hera.com or use our Live Chat option available on the website.

4. Where can I update my personal details?

Log into your account, navigate to 'Settings', and update your personal information.

5. Is my data safe with Portfolio?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data.
